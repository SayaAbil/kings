about us

Bridging Real-World Assets with Blockchain

Kings RWA is a revolutionary decentralized collateralized lending protocol focused on real-world assets (RWAs). Our platform bridges the gap between traditional income-generating assets and blockchain technology, creating a secure and high-yield lending environment for investors. We use tangible, income-generating assets such as cars, yachts, and real estate as collateral, ensuring that all investments are backed by valuable, real-world assets.

Dubai’s Premier High-End Rental Service

Our journey began with Kings Car Rental, founded in 2017, a successful Dubai-based company providing premium cars for rent. The idea for Kings Car Rental came from the founder's dissatisfaction with the existing car rental services in Dubai. The company started with just 3 cars and has since grown into a premium rental service boasting a fleet of 145 luxury vehicles, including Lamborghini Aventador S Roadster, Ferrari 812 Superfast, and McLaren 720S. Kings Car Rental is now one of the top providers of high-end rental cars, guaranteeing a premium experience for every customer.

From Luxury Rentals to Decentralized Finance

Building on this proven success, Kings RWA leverages the expertise of the same team that grew Kings Car Rental. With a strong foundation in asset management and an innovative approach to decentralized finance, we are set to redefine how real-world assets are used to create sustainable returns for investors.

Risk Management

Comprehensive Risk Mitigation Strategies

Depreciation Risk: Slightly used cars from Europe and Japan are purchased, repaired in-house, and optimized for rental income.

Operational Risk: Proven fleet management systems ensure high utilization and reduced downtime.

Insurance & Liability: Full insurance coverage protects the fleet.

Market Volatility: Serving both corporate and tourism sectors hedges against demand fluctuations.
